Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Gertruda ("we," "our," or "the App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

IMPORTANT: Gertruda is an entertainment application providing dream interpretations, compatibility readings, horoscope forecasts, and oracle-style guidance. Our services are for entertainment purposes only and should not be considered professional advice.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Profile Information: Names, birthdates, birth places (city, country - optional), zodiac signs
  • User Content: Dream descriptions, questions, compatibility queries, oracle questions and answers
  • Account Preferences: Language selection, theme preferences, notification settings
  • Purchase Information: In-app purchase receipts (processed by Apple/Google)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Features used, session duration, interaction patterns
  • Technical Data: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Device Advertising ID: Collected with your consent on iOS 14.5+ via App Tracking Transparency; used for personalized advertising
  • Analytics Data: Feature usage statistics, error logs (only if you opt-in)
  • Cache Data: Locally stored AI responses for faster performance
  • Notification Preferences: Your push notification settings and scheduled notification data

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your precise location data
  • We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or camera
  • We do NOT track you across other apps or websites (unless you grant permission via App Tracking Transparency on iOS)
  • We do NOT collect sensitive health or medical information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for:

  • Service Delivery: Generating dream interpretations, compatibility readings, horoscopes, and oracle responses
  • AI Processing: Sending your content to AI providers (Google Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic) for analysis
  • Personalization: Remembering your preferences and zodiac profile
  • Push Notifications: Sending horoscope reminders and re-engagement notifications based on your preferences
  • Advertising: Displaying relevant advertisements (with your consent for personalized ads on iOS)
  • App Improvement: Understanding feature usage and fixing bugs (with your consent)
  • Feature Management: Using Firebase Remote Config to manage app features and updates
  • Customer Support: Responding to your inquiries and technical issues
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting regulatory requirements and enforcing our Terms

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 AI Service Providers

Your content (dreams, questions, etc.) is sent to third-party AI providers for processing:

  • Google Gemini (Primary AI Provider) - Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI (Alternative Provider) - Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Alternative Provider) - Privacy Policy

These providers process your content to generate responses. The app uses automatic failover between providers to ensure service availability.

3.2 Google Services

We use the following Google services:

  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymized usage data to help us understand how users interact with the app (optional, can be disabled in Settings)
  • Firebase Remote Config: Manages feature flags and app configuration (e.g., force update requirements, ad settings)
  • Google AdMob: Displays banner advertisements in the app

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

3.3 Advertising

We display banner advertisements through Google AdMob:

  • iOS Users: On iOS 14.5 and later, we request your permission via App Tracking Transparency before collecting your device advertising ID for personalized ads
  • If you decline tracking: You will receive non-personalized advertisements
  • Ad display: Banner ads appear in the Settings screen
  • Ad control: We can enable or disable ads remotely via Firebase Remote Config

3.4 Payment Processors

Purchase information is handled by:

  • Apple App Store (for iOS users) - Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Store (for Android users) - Privacy Policy

We do NOT receive or store your credit card information.

3.5 Analytics Services (Optional)

If you enable analytics, anonymized usage data may be shared with Firebase Analytics. You can disable this at any time in Settings.

3.6 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental request, or to protect our rights and safety.

3.7 No Sale of Data

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Dreams, horoscopes, compatibility readings, and oracle responses are saved in a local encrypted database
  • User profiles (including names, birthdates, birth places, zodiac signs) are stored locally
  • Preferences and settings are stored in device-specific storage
  • Cached responses are stored locally to improve performance
  • Notification schedules are stored locally for push notification delivery

4.2 Security Measures

  • HTTPS encryption for all network communications
  • Secure storage using iOS Keychain and Android Keystore for API keys
  • No sensitive data transmitted in plain text
  • Regular security updates and vulnerability testing
  • No server-side storage of your personal content

4.3 Data Retention

  • Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Analytics data (if enabled) is retained per Google's data retention policies
  • AI queries are processed in real-time and are not stored by us
  • Cached data is automatically cleared based on your cache settings

5. Push Notifications

We offer push notifications to enhance your experience:

  • Horoscope Reminders: Daily reminders about your horoscope readings
  • Re-engagement Notifications: Notifications sent on Day 1, 3, and 7 if you haven't used the app
  • User Control: You can enable or disable notifications in Settings or through your device's system settings
  • OS Permissions: Notifications require your permission through your device's operating system

6. Force Updates

The app may require updates for security, compatibility, or feature reasons:

  • Version Control: We use Firebase Remote Config to manage minimum app version requirements
  • Update Prompts: If your app version is below the required minimum, you will be prompted to update
  • Store Links: Update prompts direct you to the Apple App Store or Google Play Store
