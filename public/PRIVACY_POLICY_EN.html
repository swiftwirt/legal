Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 4, 2026

App: Gertruda (iOS & Android)

Welcome to Gertruda ("we", "us", or "the App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Personal Data: To generate personalized horoscopes and forecasts, we collect your name and date of birth.
  • Feature Inputs: Text descriptions of dreams for the Dream Interpreter, specific questions for the Magic Oracle, and birth data for Love Compatibility checks.
  • Preferences: Your selected language (English, German, French, Spanish, Kazakh, or Portuguese) and notification settings.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Technical Data: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for session management.
  • Infrastructure Logs: Basic connection metadata processed via Firebase for hosting (gertruda.fun) and app stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly to provide the App's entertainment features:

  • To generate personalized horoscopes and forecasts.
  • To provide compatibility scores and dream interpretations.
  • To manage App configuration via Firebase Remote Config.
  • To send scheduled re-engagement notifications (if enabled).

3. Third-Party Services

We utilize trusted third-party services to deliver technical functionality:

  • Firebase (Google): For app infrastructure, hosting of the landing page, and remote configuration management.
  • AI Analytical APIs: To process user text inputs (dreams and oracle questions) in real-time to generate responses.
  • App Stores: Apple and Google handle all in-app purchase transactions securely.

4. Data Storage & Security

Local Storage: Most personal data, including your horoscope profile, is stored directly on your device. We do not maintain a permanent centralized database of your private dream descriptions or oracle questions.

Cloud Processing: While inputs are processed in the cloud for AI analysis, we prioritize ephemeral processing over permanent storage.
